About Air Dust Odour

Chartered environmental professionals delivering expert air quality, dust, odour, noise and lighting services with integrity and precision.

Our Story

Expertise Built on Experience

Air Dust Odour was founded with a clear mission: to provide accessible, high-quality environmental consultancy services across the UK. With over a decade of experience in air quality assessment, our chartered professionals understand the complexities of environmental planning and regulation.

We work with developers, architects, planners, construction companies and local authorities to deliver the environmental assessments that keep projects moving. From screening assessments for small residential developments to detailed dispersion modelling for major infrastructure projects, we bring the same commitment to accuracy and professionalism.

Our approach is straightforward: we listen to what you need, provide honest advice, and deliver clear, well-written reports on time. No jargon, no unnecessary upselling, just expert environmental assessment from people who know their field.
